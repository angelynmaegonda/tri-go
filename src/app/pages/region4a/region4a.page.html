Aguinaldo Shrine, Cavite
The Emilio Aguinaldo Shrine is a national shrine located in Kawit, Cavite, where the Philippine Declaration of Independence from Spain was declared on June 12, 1898.
Pagsanjan Falls, Laguna
Pagsanjan Falls, also known as Cavinti Falls (local name: Magdapio Falls), is one of the most famous waterfalls in the country, located in Laguna.
Taal Volcano, Batangas
Taal Volcano is a large caldera filled by Taal Lake located in the province of Batangas.
