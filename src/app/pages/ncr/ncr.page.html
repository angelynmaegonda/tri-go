NATIONAL CAPITAL REGION
Malacañang Palace, Manila
Malacañang Palace, officially known as Malacañan Palace, is the official residence and principal workplace of the president of the Philippines.
Rizal Park, Manila
Rizal Park, also known as Luneta Park, is a historic urban park located in Ermita, Manila.
Intramuros, Manila
Intramuros (Latin for "inside the walls") is the 0.67-square-kilometer historic walled area within the city of Manila.
