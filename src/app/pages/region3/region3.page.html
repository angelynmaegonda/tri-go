CENTRAL LUZON
Subic Bay
Subic Bay is a bay on the west coast of the island of Luzon surrounded by Subic and Olongapo City (Zambales), and Morong, Bataan.
Mount Pinatubo, Zambales
Mount Pinatubo is an active stratovolcano in the Zambales Mountains, located on the tripoint boundary of the provinces of Zambales, Tarlac, and Pampanga.
Dambana ng Kagitingan, Bataan
Mount Samat National Shrine is a historical shrine located near the summit of Mount Samat in the town of Pilar, Bataan.
