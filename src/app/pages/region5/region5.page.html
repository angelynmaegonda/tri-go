Mayon Volcano, Albay
Mayon Volcano, also known as Mount Mayon, is an active stratovolcano in the province of Albay.
Cagsawa Ruins, Albay
The Cagsawa Ruins are the remnants of a 16th-century Franciscan church, the Cagsawa Church, located in Daraga, Albay.
Subic Beach, Sorsogon
Subic Beach is a pinkish-white sand beach composed of two segments located in the island of Calintaan off Matnog, Sorsogon.
