Subic Bay

Its shores were formerly the site of a major United States Navy facility, U.S. Naval Base Subic Bay, converted into a beach town, now an industrial and commercial area known as the Subic Bay Freeport Zone.

Today, water as well as the towns and establishments surrounding the bay are collectively known as Subic Bay.

The bay is long recognized for its deep and protected waters, but developments were slow due to lack of level terrain around the bay.

Scuba diving is a very popular Subic tourist attraction. Subic Bay offers some of the world's best shipwrecks, all within recreational diving depths, as well as tranquil coral and artificial reefs that explode with marine life.
