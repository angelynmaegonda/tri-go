WELCOME TO TRI-GO!

Tri-Go! is a virtual adventure app that provides you with interesting facts about the countless places located all around the Philippines.


From Luzon, Visayas, to Mindanao, Tri-Go! will take you wherever you want to go in the country with just a click of a button.


This informative app includes different articles with varying topic that you can read for fun and, at the same time, boost your knowledge of the local geography!
